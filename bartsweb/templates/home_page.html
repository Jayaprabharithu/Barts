We create architectural designs

An architectural concept serves as the foundation for a design project. It’s the seed from which the completed building or structure grows. These concepts guide an architect’s decisions, shaping the project’s evolution. They can emerge from various sources, such as personal experiences, emotions, research, and analysis

Modern Architecture

Modern architecture is a style of building that emphasizes function and a streamlined form over ornamentation. This design aesthetic began in the 1930s as a departure from more elaborate and decorated homes like Queen Anne, Victorian, or Gothic Revival styles. Modern architecture, which was most often seen through the 1970s, usually involves sharp, clean lines.
